Tool 05 — Integrated

Billthrough, Declines & Retry ROI

Tools 01 and 02 unified into a single causal model — decline mix directly drives billthrough rate, and a retry ROI calculator determines whether each decline type is worth the cost of retrying. Reduce config errors and watch billthrough rise in real time. Set retry cost and success rate to see net revenue impact after retry spend.

This tool embodies a core analytical principle: declines and billthrough are not separate metrics — they are two sides of the same equation. The model makes that causal relationship interactive and quantifies the economics of recovery at each step.
