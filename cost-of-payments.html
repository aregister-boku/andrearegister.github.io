Cost of Payments Diagnostic

Most payment teams know their authorization rate. Few know their true Cost of Payments in basis points — or how much they are overpaying relative to benchmark. This tool diagnoses your CoP across PSP fees, interchange, tokenization, and retry waste, and ranks every savings opportunity by annual impact.

A 10 bps reduction in CoP at $100M annual volume is $100K back to the P&L. At $1B it is $1M. This is the metric every CFO understands but most payment teams cannot yet produce.
