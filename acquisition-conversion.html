Acquisition Conversion Diagnostic

Where is revenue leaking at checkout — before customers become subscribers or buyers? This tool diagnoses conversion gaps by payment method, market, and funnel stage, and quantifies the annual revenue opportunity from closing each one.

Auth rate measures whether a payment attempt succeeded. Conversion rate measures whether a customer completed checkout at all. These are different problems with different interventions — and most teams only measure one.
